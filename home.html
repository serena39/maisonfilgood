FILGood 25AW "RAW SILHOUETTE" Collection
By Stylist Fil Xiaobai

Stripping away all redundancy, returning to clothing's essence. The FILGood 2025 Autumn/Winter collection takes "Unfinished Completion" as its theme, deconstructing traditional uniform language through precision-cut wool and heavyweight cotton foundations.

The signature asymmetric single-pleat trousers continue the architectural tailoring, with deliberately unfinished thread ends at the back waist becoming the collection's signature detail. Newly developed concrete gray dyeing technology allows coats to exhibit dynamic wrinkles in static poses. This season's debut 3D-knit dress outlines the golden ratio of the human body through singular lines.

"True minimalism isn't about subtraction,
but absolute mastery of essential elements"
— Fil Xiaobai
The 23-piece collection will debut on September 1st at the concept store on Yuyuan Road, Shanghai, accompanied by an immersive knot installation art exhibition.

FILGood "ESSENTIAL ARCHITECTURE" Capsule
Curated by Stylist Fil Xiaobai

The FILGood 25SS/24AW capsule collection distills urban sophistication into 12 genderless silhouettes. Featuring Japanese technical fabric with organic cotton blends, each piece embodies the brand's philosophy of "wearable architecture."

Standouts include the convertible trench with hidden magnetic closures and the zero-waste draped top that transforms through strategic knotting. The monochromatic palette - cement grey, oxidized silver, and chalk white - reflects FILGood's signature industrial minimalism.

"A capsule should be like a good algorithm -
maximum function with minimum elements"
— Fil Xiaobai
Available exclusively at Dover Street Market locations from April 30th, with select pieces featured in the brand's Shanghai flagship installation.
